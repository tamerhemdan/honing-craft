About Honingcraft

THE HONINGCRAFT COMMITMENT TO QUALITY AND SERVICE

Honingcraft supplies honed tubing and chrome bar from South Africa. We support hydraulic cylinder manufacturers with materials and surface finishing technology. Our massive stock and variety of precision free cutting steel makes Honingcraft a preferred supplier to the best hydraulic manufacturers in Africa. Honingcraft provides honing services which ensure that every barrel is honed to the finest tolerances of smoothness, concentricity and parallelism. Honingcraft operates from a 10,000 m2 factory serviced by a combined 100 tonnes of cranage and a staff complement of 60 employees. We provide precision surface treatment services through our two partner companies Honingcraft Moser and Honingcraft Jünger.

Honingcraft Moser specialises in hard chrome plating by electrolysis and precision internal and external grinding to extend the effective lifespan of specialised components like hydraulic cylinder rods, rock drills, mono-pump shafts, automotive press dies, air valves, locomotive engine liners and ships engine liners.

Honingcraft Jünger provides similar chroming and precision grinding services in Port Elizabeth. We carry stock of tubing, chrome bar and cylinder kits for local distribution. Honingcraft Jünger provides metal spray as an additional service.
